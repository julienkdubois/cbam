================================================================
DATASET: EU27 vs Rest of World (RoW) — Bilateral Dynare Panel
Output file: dynare_data_bilateral_eu_row.csv
================================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
This dataset mirrors the structure of the EU27/USA bilateral panel
(dynare_data_bilateral_2002Q1_2019Q4.csv) but replaces the USA with
an aggregate Rest of World (RoW) block. It contains 11 observables,
named identically to the bilateral case: h = home = EU27, f = foreign = RoW.


OBSERVABLES
-----------

obs_dy_h, obs_dy_f — Real GDP growth, quarter-on-quarter (%)
  EU27 : taken directly from the OECD quarterly national accounts
         (TRANSACTION = B1GQ, seasonally adjusted, growth rate G1).
  RoW  : GDP levels of all RoW countries are SUMMED each quarter,
         then QoQ % change is computed on that aggregate.
         Rationale: avoids bias from averaging individual growth rates
         when country sizes differ.

obs_dc_h, obs_dc_f — Real consumption growth, quarter-on-quarter (%)
  EU27 : final consumption expenditure (P3) seasonally adjusted,
         deflated by SA CPI, then QoQ % change.
  RoW  : consumption levels are SUMMED across RoW countries, the
         aggregate is deflated by a GDP-weighted aggregate CPI index,
         then QoQ % change is computed on the deflated aggregate.

obs_pi_h, obs_pi_f — CPI inflation, quarter-on-quarter (%)
  EU27 : QoQ % change of the seasonally adjusted CPI index (OECD).
  RoW  : QoQ % change of a GDP-weighted average CPI index across
         RoW countries, after seasonal adjustment of the aggregate.

obs_r_h, obs_r_f — Short-term interest rate, quarterly (%)
  EU27 : 3-month Euribor / ECB deposit facility rate (annualised),
         divided by 4 to convert to a quarterly rate.
  RoW  : GDP-weighted mean of country short-term rates (annualised),
         divided by 4. Weighting reflects the relative size of each
         RoW economy in the aggregate.

obs_de — Real exchange rate, LEVEL index (base = 100)
  Source: Eurostat CPI-based REER for EU27, IC37/IC41 basket.
  The EU27 REER is already a bilateral real exchange rate of the
  EU27 against its non-EU trading partners — which is exactly the
  RoW definition used here. It is therefore used directly as a
  level index without any further transformation.
  A rise in obs_de means a real appreciation of the EU27 vs RoW.
  NOTE: unlike the bilateral EU27/USA dataset where obs_de is a
  QoQ change, here obs_de is kept as a LEVEL index to allow
  Dynare measurement equations written in levels.

obs_tb_h — Trade balance of EU27, % of EU27 GDP
  Source: OECD trade balance (goods + services, current USD billions,
  COUNTERPART_AREA = World). Converted to % of EU27 GDP in USD millions
  (×1000 to harmonise units).

obs_tb_f — Trade balance of RoW, % of RoW GDP
  Sum of individual country trade balances (OECD, vs World) for all
  RoW countries present in the panel, expressed as % of total RoW GDP.
  This does NOT cover the entire world — only the 25 countries listed
  below. Partial coverage is accepted by construction.


REST OF WORLD — COUNTRY LIST
-----------------------------
RoW is defined as the non-EU countries in the Eurostat IC37/IC41
REER basket. All 27 EU member states are excluded by construction,
including Denmark and Sweden (EU members, non-euro area).

The following 28 countries are defined as the RoW basket:

  USA  — United States          JPN  — Japan
  GBR  — United Kingdom         CHE  — Switzerland
  NOR  — Norway                 ISL  — Iceland
  CAN  — Canada                 AUS  — Australia
  NZL  — New Zealand            CHN  — China
  KOR  — South Korea            HKG  — Hong Kong
  SGP  — Singapore *            IDN  — Indonesia
  IND  — India                  THA  — Thailand *
  MYS  — Malaysia *             BRA  — Brazil
  MEX  — Mexico                 ARG  — Argentina
  CHL  — Chile                  COL  — Colombia
  CRI  — Costa Rica             TUR  — Turkey
  RUS  — Russia                 ZAF  — South Africa
  SAU  — Saudi Arabia           ISR  — Israel

  * SGP, THA, MYS are in the theoretical basket but are absent from
    the panel (no OECD data available). They are excluded from all
    aggregations. The 25 countries effectively used are all others above.

Countries excluded from RoW (EU members):
  AUT BEL BGR CYP CZE DEU DNK ESP EST FIN FRA GRC HRV HUN IRL
  ITA LTU LUX LVA MLT NLD POL PRT ROU SVK SVN SWE


UNITS SUMMARY
-------------
  obs_dy_h / obs_dy_f  : percent (QoQ growth rate)
  obs_dc_h / obs_dc_f  : percent (QoQ growth rate)
  obs_pi_h / obs_pi_f  : percent (QoQ inflation)
  obs_r_h  / obs_r_f   : percent (quarterly rate = annualised / 4)
  obs_de               : index level, base = 100 (reference year of Eurostat REER)
  obs_tb_h / obs_tb_f  : percent of own GDP


SAMPLE PERIOD
-------------
  Starts : 2002-Q1 (same as bilateral EU27/USA panel)
  Ends   : last quarter with complete observations across all 11 variables
